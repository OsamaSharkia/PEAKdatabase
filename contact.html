Universities in Kent

Kent is home to several excellent universities. Here are the main institutions where we can help you access higher education:

University of Kent

Main Campus: Canterbury, Kent, CT2 7NZ

Additional Campus: Medway Campus, Chatham Maritime

Known For: Wide range of academic programs, strong research focus, and dedicated support for refugee students through their Sanctuary Scholarships.

Visit Website Open in Google Maps

Canterbury Christ Church University

Main Campus: North Holmes Road, Canterbury, Kent, CT1 1QU

Additional Campuses: Medway Campus (Chatham Maritime), Tunbridge Wells

Known For: Strong teaching programs, health and social care courses, and inclusive approach to education with dedicated Sanctuary Awards for refugees.

Visit Website Open in Google Maps

University for the Creative Arts (UCA)

Kent Campuses: Canterbury (New Dover Road, CT1 3AN) and Rochester (Fort Pitt, ME1 1DZ)

Known For: Specialist creative arts education, including art, design, architecture, media, and creative business courses. Offers portfolio-based admissions which can be helpful for those with non-traditional qualifications.
